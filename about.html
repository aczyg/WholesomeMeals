Our Story

WholesomeMeals was born for the busy students who needs access to healthy and nutritious meals in school.

We're a one-stop food ordering website that offers our customers healthy and nutritious meals that are affordable and delicious, prepared for grab-and-go, and delivered ready-to-eat through our vending machines by pre-ordering on our website.

We provide our customers the opportunity to pre-order our meals via our website, pay and then collect through our vending machines.
